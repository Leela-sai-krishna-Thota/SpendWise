# GTM.md (Go-to-Market Strategy)

This document maps out a hyper-tactical, zero-budget Go-To-Market strategy designed to acquire the first 100 high-intent startup leads for the AI Spend Audit tool within 30 days.

---

## 1. The Exact Target User
Our target user is not a generalized "startup", but the **Head of Engineering** or **VP of Engineering** at an **early-stage SaaS startup (Seed to Series A)** with a team size of 8–35 people. 

- **Why this profile?** At this stage, they are growing quickly, and their engineers are independently spinning up AI seats (Cursor, Claude, Copilot, ChatGPT Pro). They have no formal IT procurement, but their runway is shortening and the CEO is demanding cost controls. They are senior enough to change the tool stack but close enough to the codebase to care about the friction of moving tools.

## 2. Pre-audit Behavior (What They Scroll or Google)
Right before they realize they need a spend auditor, they are searching for:
- "Cursor pro slow requests limit bypassed"
- "Force GitHub Copilot to use Claude 3.5 Sonnet"
- "Claude Team plan minimum 5 seats billing hack"
- "How to stop OpenAI api bill from exploding in dev staging"
- "v0.dev pricing vs Claude artifact limits"

They are also scrolling through GitHub issues discussing IDE alternatives or complaining about Anthropic’s rate limits on X.

## 3. High-Concentration Online Hubs
Instead of shouting into the void, we will insert ourselves into high-density developer operations and bootstrapping hubs:
- **Subreddits**: `r/ExperiencedDevs` (focused on engineering standards), `r/selfhosted`, and `r/saas`.

## 4. Building Traction: Zero-Budget 30-Day Blueprint (First 100 Users)
We will bypass generic SEO blog posts (which take 6 months to rank) and focus on hyper-targeted, high-value outbound engineering:

1. **The "Shadow Audit" Outbound (Days 1–10) - *First 20 Users***:
   - Identify 50 Series-A startups currently listing open software engineering roles.
   - Look up their VP of Engineering on LinkedIn.
   - Send a highly personalized 3-sentence DM: *"Hey [Name], noticing you are scaling your team with Cursor. Built an instant audit calculator specifically to surface these overlaps. Check it out at [URL] - no login required."*
2. **The "Free Value Drop" on Hacker News (Days 11–15) - *Next 30 Users***:

3. **The "Reverse Portfolio" VC Outreach (Days 16–30) - *Next 50 Users***:
   - Identify 10 mid-tier VC funds who list their seed companies publicly (e.g., YC, Techstars, First Round).
   - Email the fund's **Operating Partner** / **Head of Platform**: *"We built a 60-second AI Tooling Spend auditor. Portfolio support teams are sending this to their seed CEOs to instantly clip $200–$800/mo off their SaaS bill. Want a bulk white-glove report for your active seed companies? Here’s the calculator link."*
