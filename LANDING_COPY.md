## 3. FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

### Q1: Do I need to connect our corporate billing or log in?
> **A**: No. To preserve your security and maintain easy compliance, we require zero OAuth connections or database logins. You simply select the tools and seat counts you pay for, and our engine calculates the exact spend optimization paths locally.

### Q2: How does the savings engine work?
> **A**: The engine reviews standard vendor contracts, licensing floors (like Claude Team’s 5-seat requirement or ChatGPT Team’s 2-seat minimum), and functional overlaps (e.g., using both Cursor and Copilot simultaneously) to find redundancies and downgrade pathways.

### Q3: What is Credex, and why are consultations offered?
> **A**: Credex is an AI infrastructure company that brokers secondary-market AI credits. If you find high spend (> $500/mo), Credex can help transition your developer subscriptions into discount pools, saving up to 45% compared to retail prices.

### Q4: Is our data secure and private?
> **A**: Absolutely. All input states are handled locally on your browser. When you share an audit report via a unique URL, all identifying team and company details are stripped from the URL payload and public views.

### Q5: How often is the pricing data updated?
> **A**: Our database is synced weekly. Every cost estimate traces directly back to the verified, public pricing pages of OpenAI, Anthropic, Cursor, and Vercel. Refer to our public `PRICING_DATA.md` repository for the complete list of verified URLs.
